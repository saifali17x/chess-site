About CFP

Promoting chess excellence across Pakistan

The Chess Federation of Pakistan (CFP) is dedicated to promoting and developing chess as a mind sport throughout Pakistan. We believe in the power of chess to enhance cognitive abilities, strategic thinking, and character development.

Our mission is to make chess accessible to everyone, especially students, and to create opportunities for players to compete at national and international levels.

With Pakistan's youth chess scene flourishing, the future of the sport looks promising, with many of our players poised to compete on national and international stages in the years to come.
